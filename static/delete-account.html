Delete Your prayerloop Account

How to permanently delete your account and associated data

Option 1 — Delete in the app (fastest)

If you still have prayerloop installed, you can delete your account immediately:

  1. Open the prayerloop app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Your account and data are removed right away — no waiting period.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you've already uninstalled the app or can't sign in, email us from the address associated with your account and we'll process the deletion for you:

Email support@prayerloop.io

Please send your request from, or include, the email address on your account so we can verify it. We will complete the deletion within 30 days of a verified request.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • Your user profile and login credentials
  • Prayers you created
  • Your prayer history, sessions, and usage statistics
  • Your app preferences and notification settings
  • Your group memberships and any group invites you created
  • Push notification tokens and password-reset tokens

Note: prayers you shared into a group remain visible to the other members of that group, as they belong to the shared group rather than to your personal account. Your personal account and personal data are fully removed.

Data retention

After deletion, your data is removed from our active systems. Limited records may persist briefly in encrypted backups before being overwritten on our normal backup-rotation schedule. We do not retain personal data longer than required for legal or operational obligations.
